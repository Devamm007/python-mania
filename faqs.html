Frequently Asked Questions

What is the format of the final Evaluation Test?

It will be a 1 to 1.5-hour test worth 50 marks. It includes 12 MCQs (2 marks each) , 5 coding tasks (4 marks each) , and one hard question (6 marks).

How can I get a certificate?

We offer two types of certificates. Participation Certificate: Requires at least 60% attendance and timely completion of DPPs. Competition Certificate: Awarded to the top 10 winners of the competition.

When does Phase 2 start?

Phase 2 begins immediately after Quiz 2. The exact dates will be announced then.

What will the project competition involve?

You will work with a small dataset to perform data cleaning , generate summary statistics or visualizations , and submit a report with your Jupyter Notebook.

How is my final grade calculated?

The final score is weighted average: 0.2*DPP + 0.5*Test + 0.3*Project
